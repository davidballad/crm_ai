Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026  ·  Effective date: April 1, 2026

Clienta AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and their end users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our cloud-based CRM platform and related services (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Account and registration data

  • Business name, owner name, and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Billing information (processed securely through our payment provider)
  • Login credentials (passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text)

Business and customer data you provide

  • Customer contact records, purchase history, and visit logs entered into the CRM
  • Campaign content, promotional messages, and audience segments
  • Inventory items, product catalog data, and sales transactions
  • WhatsApp conversations and message logs routed through the platform

Usage and technical data

  • Log files, IP addresses, browser type, and device identifiers
  • Pages visited, features used, and session timestamps
  • Performance metrics and error reports

AI-generated data

  • Aggregated analytics, business insights, and recommendations generated by our AI models based on your business data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Generate AI-powered business insights tailored to your business
  • Send transactional communications (invoices, password resets, service notices)
  • Deliver WhatsApp and email marketing campaigns on your behalf
  • Improve the Service through aggregate, anonymized usage analysis
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal data or your customers' data to third parties. We do not use your business data to train shared AI models without your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription:

  • Your account data remains accessible for 30 days to allow for export
  • After 30 days, data is permanently deleted from our production systems
  • Backups are purged within 90 days of account closure
  • Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Ecuadorian tax law

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Multi-tenant data isolation — your data is logically separated from other customers
  • Role-based access controls and least-privilege architecture
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and dependencies

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information using commercially reasonable means.

Your Rights

As a Clienta AI customer, you have the following rights with respect to your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your customer and business data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@clientaai.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and authentication state. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how the Service is used. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings; however, this may affect functionality.

Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for businesses and is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
